LAMP PC · Private Client

Premium Advisory

Full-service real estate advisory and asset management for institutions, family offices, and UHNW families. LAMP PC delivers a disciplined six-phase optimization process that organizes, strategizes, values, plans, projects, and executes across your entire portfolio.

The Six-Phase Optimization Process

Phase 1: Organize & Structure

Digitize and centralize all property data — title reports, zoning, insurance, property management records — into a cloud-based profile for each asset.

Phase 2: Strategize

Develop a customized strategy for each asset — buy, sell, hold, finance, develop, syndicate, or renovate — aligned with portfolio goals.

Phase 3: Value

Comprehensive valuations using appraisers, AI tools, national broker network, and proprietary models across all property types.

Phase 4: Highest & Best Use

Identify and model the optimal use for each property with detailed budgets and implementation roadmaps.

Phase 5: After-Renovated Value

Project post-improvement values to guide renovation, refinancing, and investment decisions with precision.

Phase 6: Execute & Reassess

Monitor performance and continuously refine strategies — ensuring optimal outcomes over time through ongoing portfolio management.
