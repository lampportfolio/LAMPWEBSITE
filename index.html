Three Simple Steps
How LAMP Works

Three steps to unlock institutional-grade intelligence for your entire real estate portfolio. LAMP it. Visit LAMPit.ai to get started.

1
Login & Add Properties
Create your free account at LAMPit.ai. Add your property addresses to build your portfolio. Then build your real estate advisory boardroom — share per property or your entire portfolio with one easy invite to assemble your advisory team of CPAs, wealth managers, brokers, and more.
2
"Light Up" Your Portfolio
Each property shows a Strategy section with action buttons — Sell, Exchange, Improve, Hold, Finance, Premium, and Purchase. Click the solution you need and hit the action button. LAMP distributes your inquiry to the appropriate LAMP Link Network Partner. You also get Alerts / Insights and Strategy Resources surfaced per property.

Connect & Transact
When you light up a solution, your existing advisors in that category are notified first — your realtor gets the sell inquiry, your CPA gets the tax question. LAMP Link Network Partners only step in when there's no advisor already in that role. LAMP is a marketplace for the gaps, not a replacement for the relationships you already have. All platform and referral fees are paid by Network Partners — never by you.

Your Data, Your Control
Built on Trust, Transparency & Security

Your portfolio data is private by default. No one — not advisors, not Network Partners, not LAMP — can view your information unless you explicitly choose to share it. And LAMP will never provide legal, financial, tax, or real estate advice of any kind.

Data Ownership Statement

Property owners own their data. Entirely. Always.

Nothing in your portfolio is visible to anyone — not your advisors, not LAMP Link Network Partners, not LAMP itself — until you make an explicit choice to share it. You control what is shared, with whom, and when. You can revoke access at any time from your Settings tab.

LAMP is a technology platform and marketplace. We do not provide legal, financial, tax, investment, or real estate advice of any kind. We do not hold licenses as a broker, lender, attorney, or financial advisor. Our role is to organize your data, surface intelligence, and facilitate connections. All professional services are delivered by independent LAMP Link Network Partners or your own existing advisors — not by LAMP.

Private by Default
Your property data belongs to you and is invisible to everyone until you take action. Share your entire portfolio or individual properties with advisors you choose. When you click "Inquire Now," your inquiry goes to LAMP. We connect you with the appropriate LAMP Link Network Partner, and you decide if and when to share your data with them.
Visibility Only When You Share
When you choose to share, your dashboard shows exactly how many advisors have access. You can always manage who you've shared with in your Settings tab. Add or remove access at any time.
Outside the Regulatory Sandbox
LAMP is not a licensed broker, lender, financial advisor, or legal counsel. We intentionally operate as a neutral technology platform and marketplace — organizing data and facilitating connections without crossing into regulated advisory territory. All licensed professional services are provided by independent Network Partners.
Enterprise-Grade Protection
Encryption in transit and at rest, strong access controls, regular vulnerability scanning, incident response planning, and vendor risk assessments — all built on SOC 2-inspired best practices. Formal SOC 2 Type 1 certification is in progress.
Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
Is LAMP really free for property owners? +
Yes — LAMP is completely free for property owners and investors. Sign up, log in, and use the platform at no cost. No subscriptions, no hidden fees. We are a marketplace. Only subscribing advisors (LAMP Link Network Partners) pay to access shared portfolios and participate in the network.
What is LAMP exactly? +
LAMP is your AI-powered real estate command center — delivering institutional-grade intelligence and clear visibility into every property you own. Analyze assets, uncover opportunities to sell, exchange, finance, improve, or optimize, and connect seamlessly to our marketplace of real estate solutions and LAMP Link Network Partners.
What happens when I "Light Up" a property? +
When you click a solution button (Sell, Exchange, Finance, Improve, or Premium) and hit "Inquire Now," LAMP first notifies any existing advisors you've assigned to that category in your Advisory Boardroom. Your realtor gets the sell inquiry. Your CPA gets the tax question. LAMP Link Network Partners only receive the referral when no advisor has been assigned to that category — filling the gap, not replacing a relationship.
Will LAMP replace my existing advisors or interfere with those relationships? +
No — and this is by design. LAMP is built to strengthen your existing advisory relationships, not compete with them. When you light up a solution, your advisors in that category are notified first. A LAMP Link Network Partner only steps in when there's no advisor already assigned to that role. Think of LAMP Link as a marketplace for the gaps in your advisory team — not a replacement for the professionals you already trust.
What types of properties can I add? +
LAMP supports a wide range of property types — single family homes, multifamily, commercial, retail, industrial, and mixed-use. Simply enter the property address and LAMP's AI-powered platform handles the rest.
Does LAMP provide legal, financial, or real estate advice? +
No. LAMP provides no legal, financial, tax, or real estate advice. We do not offer brokerage, lending, consulting, or any professional services. Our role is purely to facilitate connections in a secure, data-rich environment.
Who can see my property data? +
No one — not advisors, not LAMP Link Network Partners, not LAMP itself — can see your data unless you explicitly grant access. Your portfolio is completely private by default. You can always view and manage who you've given permissions to in your LAMPit.ai account under the Settings tab.
Who are LAMP Link Network Partners and how do they join? +
LAMP Link Network Partners are real estate professionals — brokers, exchange sponsors, lenders, attorneys, wealth managers, construction managers, and private client advisors. For advisors, LAMP Link functions as a real estate advisory CRM where client assets are the trackable entity. They join when a property owner invites them to view a shared portfolio. Their 30-day free trial begins automatically on first access to shared content.
What happens after the advisor's 30-day free trial? +
At the end of the 30-day free trial, advisors are prompted to review and electronically sign the LAMP Link Network Partner Terms of Service and enter payment information via Stripe to continue access.

LAMP Link Solo — $49/mo (or $45/mo billed annually). For individual advisors who want full platform access and a LAMP Link Network.

LAMP Link Teams — $35/seat/mo, auto-detected when 3 or more advisors join from the same firm. Saves 29% vs. Solo.

Enterprise — Custom pricing for firms that need white-label integration, API access, or volume licensing. Contact info@lampportfolio.com.

Property owners are always free — no subscription, no credit card required.
Are there any fees beyond the monthly subscription? +
Yes. LAMP Link Network Partners are subject to two additional fees beyond their monthly subscription:

Referral Fee — $199
Charged per referral sent when a property owner with no existing advisor in a given category clicks "Inquire Now" and LAMP routes the inquiry to a Network Partner. This fee applies per referral delivered, regardless of outcome.

Technology Fee — $199
Charged upon a completed transaction where LAMP was the originating referral source — for example, a property sale, a completed 1031 exchange, a closed refinance, or any other executed transaction. This fee applies in addition to the referral fee where both are applicable, meaning a LAMP-referred deal that closes may carry up to $398 in total LAMP fees.

Neither fee applies to transactions within an advisor's existing shared portfolio relationships — only to LAMP-originated referrals. LAMP reserves the right to audit completed transactions per the terms of the LAMP Link Network Partner Terms of Service, which all Network Partners sign upon activation.
How do I share my portfolio with my advisors? +
After adding your properties, click "Share Portfolio" and enter the email addresses of your advisory team. If you'd prefer to share selectively, you can expand on any individual property and share just that one asset. Advisors receive view-only access; they cannot modify your data.
How does LAMP protect my data? +
Our practices include strong access controls, encryption in transit and at rest, regular vulnerability scanning, incident response planning, and vendor risk assessments — all built on SOC 2-aligned best practices. We are actively implementing SOC 2 Type 1 controls and expect formal certification in the near future.
