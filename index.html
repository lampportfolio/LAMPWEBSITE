LAMP PC · Private Client

Premium Advisory

Full-service real estate advisory and asset management for institutions, family offices, and UHNW families. LAMP PC delivers a disciplined six-phase optimization process that organizes, strategizes, values, plans, projects, and executes across your entire portfolio.

The Six-Phase Optimization Process

Phase 1: Organize & Structure

Digitize and centralize all property data — title reports, zoning, insurance, property management records — into a cloud-based profile for each asset.

Phase 2: Strategize

Develop a customized strategy for each asset — buy, sell, hold, finance, develop, syndicate, or renovate — aligned with portfolio goals.

Phase 3: Value

Comprehensive valuations using appraisers, AI tools, national broker network, and proprietary models across all property types.

Phase 4: Highest & Best Use

Identify and model the optimal use for each property with detailed budgets and implementation roadmaps.

Phase 5: After-Renovated Value

Project post-improvement values to guide renovation, refinancing, and investment decisions with precision.

Phase 6: Execute & Reassess

Monitor performance and continuously refine strategies — ensuring optimal outcomes over time through ongoing portfolio management.

Advisor Onboarding & Pricing

LAMP Network Partners are real estate professionals — brokers, exchange sponsors, lenders, attorneys, wealth managers, construction managers, and private client advisors. For advisors, LAMP functions as a real estate advisory CRM where client assets are the trackable entity — replacing scattered Excel docs and file cabinets with real-time portfolio intelligence. They join when a property owner invites them to view a shared portfolio. Their 30-day free trial begins automatically on first access to shared content.

At the end of the 30-day free trial, advisors are prompted to review and electronically sign the LAMP Network Partner Terms of Service (which details the compensation model) and enter payment information to continue access.

After signing the Terms of Service and providing payment information, the following structure applies:

Platform / Technology Fee (monthly subscription) — Billed based on the total number of property profiles the advisor can access across all shared portfolios:

• 0–20 properties: $49/month

• 21–99 properties: $149/month

• 100+ properties: $299/month

Referral Fee — $300 per referral, paid to LAMP by the advisor.

Transaction Fee — $200 per completed property transaction (sale, exchange, or financing) involving properties the advisor has access to via LAMP.

Property owners and investors use LAMP at no cost — all fees are structured for LAMP Network Partners (advisors). Advisors receive a generous 30-day risk-free trial, triggered only when real portfolios are shared (proving immediate value). Advisors pay only after experiencing the platform's benefits for client oversight, deal sourcing, or ongoing advisory work. Revenue aligns with real usage, successful referrals, and closed transactions — creating a true win-win marketplace ecosystem for connections and opportunities.
